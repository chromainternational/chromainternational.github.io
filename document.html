Required Documents

The following documents need to be provided by the employer for recruitment from Nepal:
Consulate Letter
This letter is issued by the employer addressing the Consulate General of the respective Embassy informing:

  • The appointment of Chroma International Pvt. Ltd. as the lawful agent of the employer.
  • Authorization to act on behalf of the employer and to carry all your Visa formalities with the Embassy.

Power of Attorney
A letter issued by the employer authorizing Chroma International Pvt. Ltd. on behalf of the employer in recruiting the required number of workers and to carry about all necessary formalities related with the respective Embassy and the related agencies of the Government of Nepal.

Agency / Service Agreement
This agreement is made between the Employer and Chroma International Pvt. Ltd. stating clearly and precisely all the terms and conditions regarding manpower supply from Nepal.

Employment Contract
Employment Contract duly signed between the Overseas Employer and the Nepalese Employee.

Demand Letter
A formal letter issued by the employer addressing Chroma International Pvt. Ltd. with full details of:

  • The job categories and number of workers
  • Monthly Salary
  • Contract Period
  • Working Hours
  • Description of all other facilities like food, accommodation, medical and air passage.
