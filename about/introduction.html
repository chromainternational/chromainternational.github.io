  • Introduction

Chroma International Pvt. Ltd.

Our recruitment agency, Chroma International, licensed by labor ministry, Government of Nepal, is a leading and upgraded supplier of manpower in Nepal. We have a team of professionals who have been trained and possess significant experience in the manpower recruitment industry. We provide recruitment services that are anchored on the philosophy of long-term partnership based on trust, integrity, efficiency and professionalism.

Our company vision is to strive to be known as the provider of highly-competent and success-driven workers. In the realm of global labor environment, we will do our best to provide our clients with the best available Nepalese manpower whose professional skills, competencies and work ethics are at par with the best in the world.

The mission of Chroma is client satisfaction. That is our ultimate goal. We will strive to provide competent manpower, which can make a difference in the quest to achieve their company’s goal. In return, we shall endeavor to encourage our clients to provide a professionally rewarding environment that recognizes merits, loyalty and dedication. We will do our best to look after the welfare of the workers we deploy anywhere in the world to make their employment a rewarding experience.

Chroma International uses state-of-art technology to provide the best service. The company maintains a mechanized Data Bank in which the bio-data of personnel in various disciplines are stored – from white-collar jobs to blue-collar jobs. Based on client’s requirement, Chroma screens candidates and presents the best short listed candidates to the client. After this, interviews of potential candidates are arranged in Kathmandu at our own spacious premises. In case of bulk recruitment, interviews can be arranges at any large center in Nepal.

Chroma undertakes the responsibility of receiving the clients at airport and arranging hotel accommodation for them. The clients can enjoy considerable discounts in their hotel bills on the basis of Chroma’s corporate relationship with them.

Chroma International manages the selected applicants until their Visa is arranged. Our duty doesn’t end up to the deployment of the candidate. We are constantly striving to make their experience a better one in every possible way we can.
